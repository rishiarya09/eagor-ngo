Mission

Our mission is to reduce the learning deficit among the promising students belonging to the underprivileged sections of the society, to help them in achieving academic excellence, and to generally raise awareness among those families about the role of good quality education in the betterment of their lives.

We pursue our mission by:

  •   Awarding cash prizes and scholarships to the qualifying students belonging to the underprivileged sections

  •   Organising after school private tuitions for the above students to complement and supplement what they learn at the schools

  •   Organising awareness camps for the families of the above sections of the society

  •   Organising counselling sessions for higher education and career

  • Promoting a sense of social responsibility among the better-off among the above sections and encouraging them to work together to reduce educational inequality
