About us

EAGOR Education Initiative is a young organisation with the noble ideology of Equality in Opportunity . Settled in Himachal Pradesh, we have begun our journey with people and partners who have the passion and courage to help the deprived and the less privileged among us. We believe with some additional support, the children belonging to these families can acquire education that can promise them a world full of opportunities and help them realise their dreams. The transformation of our society lies in the womb of knowledge and if privilege is a barrier, we aim to break every shackle of it. Ours is an initiative of the community, by the community, and for the community. Our goal is to enlighten and sensitise the students and their families about the benefits that quality of education will bring to them. We intend to pursue a combination programmes that provide both direct and indirect support to the deserving students helping them achieve academic excellence. From budding school years to college, we hand in hand with students and society, promise to deliver a niche of growth and development for the society. Taking this initiative is our moral and social responsibility. Nothing, we believe, is impossible if the idea is driven by passion and a heart full of empathy. Embedded in the three commandments of Dr. Ambedkar   Educate, Agitate and Organise ,  we emphasise the significance of the first step EDUCATION before moving on to the remaining two to transform our lives.
